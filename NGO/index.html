About us

Our Story

Harmon Charity Foundation was established out of a deep commitment to serve orphaned and vulnerable children, as well as families facing hardship. Founded in Burbank, California, USA, the Foundation was born from a passion to respond to the growing needs of children lacking access to basic necessities such as shelter, education, healthcare, and emotional support.

Through years of direct engagement with communities and individuals in need, the Foundation recognized the urgent need for a structured and transparent organization dedicated to long-term impact rather than temporary relief. This vision led to the formal establishment of Harmon Charity Foundation, guided by compassion, service, and accountability.

Despite challenges and limited resources in the early stages, our commitment to humanity has remained unwavering. With the support of generous donors, partners, and well-wishers, the Foundation continues to expand its reach and strengthen its programs aimed at restoring dignity and hope to vulnerable lives.

Our Commitment

At Harmon Charity Foundation, every contribution is handled with integrity and transparency. All donations are directed toward providing:
  • Basic necessities like food, clothing, and shelter
  • Access to education and vocational training
  • Healthcare and medical support
  • Emotional and psychological support for children and families

We are committed to making a difference in the lives of vulnerable children and families.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers

How can I donate to the foundation?

+

You can donate through the “Donate Now” buttons on each project section. All donations are securely processed and go directly to the programs supporting vulnerable children and families.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

+

Yes, Harmon Charity Foundation is a registered non-profit, and your donations are eligible for tax deductions according to local laws.

Can I volunteer or get involved?

+

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers. You can contact us via the website or social media channels to join our programs and help support the community.

Where does my donation go?

+

Your donation directly supports our initiatives including food distribution, healthcare, and educational programs for vulnerable children and families.

Can I donate for a specific project?

+

Yes! You can choose a specific project to support when donating, ensuring your contribution goes to the cause you care about most.
